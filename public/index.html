The Situation
        Education is something that is easily taken for granted. Not everyone is as lucky as others. For example, in Africa, schools and classrooms are rare and the ones that do exist often rest in impoverished areas. The students are grantful for the chance of getting a decent education, however, school supplies are limited and resources are short. A simple device such as a scientific calculator can dramatically change a student's education for the better. This is happening in third world countries on a global scale. They need our help, so let's help them!
The Vision
        GoodCalc's overall vision is to spread knowledge about the educational barrier due to the lack of school supplies and resources in developing countries all around the world. Additionally, GoodCalc strives to strongly improve the education of those students who are negatively affected by the lack of calculators and computational devices by providing them with those necessities. With a better education, we hope that each student will significantly improve his or her own life and positively impact the community as a whole.
Taking Action
        Taking action can be as simple as donating a calculator to a child in need. With your help, we can donate as many calculators as possible! Each calculation that GoodCalc makes, goes towards the education of underprivileged students around the world beginning with Africa. GoodCalc has partnered with a non-profit organization called Develop Africa Inc. in order get those calculators in the hands of the African students. Furthermore, GoodCalc is still looking for potential partners and organizations that could help distribute donated calculators to other developing nations. If you are or know of a potential partner, don't be shy and contact us! Please look at the contact information at the bottom of the page.
GoodCalc Supporters
Develop Africa: This non-profit organization is working to establish meaningful and sustainable development in Africa. Through training, scholarship, investment and partnerships Develop Africa is committed to developing African people through the promotion of transformational education, resource development, investment training and strategic empowerment. They envision an African continent fully developed in terms of its human and resource capacity, free from poverty; ignorance, and civil conflict and where the poorest and most vulnerable people become leaders and effectively manage national resources.
